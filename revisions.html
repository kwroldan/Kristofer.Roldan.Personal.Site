Revisions


I reviewed my About page and Tutorial page with my best friend and fellow code-enthusiast, Joey. We began by looking at the About page.


For the About page, the following was recommended:

  1. The sidebar should scroll with the page to remain accessible throughout.
  2. Sidebar color scheme should match the webpage.
  3. Include something personal unrelated to work.
  4. Another picture couldn't hurt!



Incorporating his feedback, I removed the white background in the header of the sidebar, changed the sidebar body background to match the rest of the site, and added the Fixed position to the sidebar, as opposed to the Absolute position.



For the Tutorial page, Joey advised the following:

  1. A table was already used for the Weapons section. A different approach would be more visually appealing for the Mods section.



I decided to ditch double-dipping on the Mods table and opted for a scroll-over effect on images with a brief description of what the mod does.
